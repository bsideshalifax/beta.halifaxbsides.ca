Halifax BSides

Halifax BSides is a non-profit organization run by members of the Halifax Technology and Information Security industry with a goal of running a yearly conference for experts, students, the curious and the confused not only to hear from others but also to provide them their chance to speak on topics that interest them. This years conference will be November 14th and look forward to sharing more with you as soon as possible.
